About Me

Hi! My name is Mohammed Rizvi and I have been a Frontend/Web Graphics developer for over 2 years. In my position I had to customize, design, and maintain web applications for clients in the events and ticketing industry. I have experience in designing responsive web pages from scratch, converting mockups and wireframes, and being able to communicate efficiently with team members and clients. I enjoy designing and structuring websites, because it allows me to use my creativity and problem solving skills. Some of the languages/technical skills I have experience in are HTML5, Pug, CSS3, Sass, Stylus, JavaScript, jQuerry, Git, Jira, and AngularJS, Ruby on Rails, and Node.js.
